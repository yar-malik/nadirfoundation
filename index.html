About the Foundation

The Nadir Foundation is a non-profit initiative committed to improving access to education for girls in remote areas. We focus on establishing and running schools where education is often a distant dream.

Our Schools

We operate girls' primary and secondary schools under our foundation. Our curriculum emphasizes English proficiency and science literacy, preparing students for a brighter future.

Our Mission

To uplift communities by educating girls in rural and underserved regions, fostering empowerment, confidence, and opportunity through quality education focused on language and scientific skills.
